4 per 1000

Reaching the 4‰ objective

The International “4 per 1000” Initiative was launched in 2021 to promote sustainable agricultural practices that enhance soil organic carbon stocks. During my PhD, I used soil carbon models to assess whether the initiative’s theoretical goal—a 4‰ annual increase in soil organic carbon stocks—was feasible at the European scale.

Main findinds from my PhD work can be found in a series of publications (Bruni et al., 2021; Bruni et al., 2022; Bruni et al., 2022; Bruni et al., 2024) and in my PhD manuscript, which is also summarized in this Holisoils blog post.
