I am part of the <a href="https://soil-modeling.org/">ISMC</a> community, which connects soil modeling experts across the world. In particular, I am involved in the <a href="https://soil-modeling.org/science-panels/working-groups/mathematics">Mathematics of Soil Processes</a> and <a href="https://soil-modeling.org/science-panels/working-groups/teaching-and-dissemination/teaching-and-dissemination">Teaching and Dissemination</a> working groups.  
In October 2023, I have been featured soil modeler on the ISMC website.
