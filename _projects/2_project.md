The <a href="https://www.facebook.com/SHARInGMeD/">SHARInG-MeD</a> project aims to build a standardised soil monitoring scheme to increase the sustainability and resilience of agriculture in Mediterranean regions.

The project involves both a soil sampling campaign, mainly in understudied drylands, and the use of models to assess soil responses to land management and drivers of soil degration. My job is to lead a working group to organize and store this data into a opensource platform, and to study the role of land-use and land-use change on soil properties with soil models.
