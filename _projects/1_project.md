<a href="https://holisoils.eu/">Holisoils</a> tackles gaps in knowledge on forest soil processes and aims to harmonise available soil monitoring information to support decision making towards climate and sustainability goals. With our group, we are building a multi-model ensemble to predict soil organic carbon stocks and greenhouse gas fluxes in European forest soils. By integrating microbial diversity into those models, we aim to improve the assessment of soil carbon changes after disturbances, such as mortality, land management and climate change.
