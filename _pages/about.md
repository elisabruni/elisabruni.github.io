Hi! Welcome to my professional webpage. I'm a scientist interested in how climate change and land management affect **soil biogeochemical cycles**.  
I use models to predict the evolution of soil organic carbon stocks and greenhouse gas fluxes in different land ecosystems, and am particularly interested in assessing how uncertainties of both model structures and parameters influence the outcomes of the simulations.  

On this website, you can find information about the projects I am involved in or collaborating on, explore my publications, and access various resources related to my academic work.
