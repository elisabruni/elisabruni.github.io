In this <a href="https://doi-org.insu.bib.cnrs.fr/10.1111/ejss.13330">paper</a>, we use a multi-model ensemble to quantify the C inputs needed to reach a 4‰ target in 16 European cropland experiments. We find that multi-model predictions show high uncertainty, even when models are calibrated to fit observed soil organic carbon stocks.

***

#### Highlights
<ul>
    <li>The feasibility of the 4‰ target was studied at 16 long-term agricultural experiments</li>
    <li>An ensemble of soil organic carbon models was used to estimate the uncertainty of the predictionsxie</li>
    <li>On average across the sites, carbon input had to increase by 119% compared to initial conditions</li>
    <li>High uncertainty of the simulations was mainly driven by water-related variables</li>
</ul>
